# MT Finance Lending Policy (Internal — Demo Only)

## 1. Buy-to-Let Mortgages

### 1.1 Eligibility
- UK residents and UK-registered SPVs (Ltd Co only, not LLP)
- Foreign nationals with indefinite leave to remain accepted; foreign nationals without ILR considered case-by-case with 65% max LTV
- Minimum applicant age: 21; maximum at application: 80
- SPV directors must personally guarantee the loan

### 1.2 Loan parameters
- Maximum LTV: 75% on standard product; 80% available on Premier product subject to enhanced criteria
- Minimum loan: £50,000
- Maximum loan: £2,000,000 per security; £5,000,000 aggregate exposure per borrower
- Terms: 2, 3, 5-year fixed; tracker available on 2-year only

### 1.3 Affordability — ICR
- Stressed ICR threshold: 140% for limited-company SPVs; 145% for personal-name BTL; 165% for higher-rate taxpayers
- Stress rate: pay rate + 2% or 5.5% whichever is higher; minimum 7.5% currently
- Rental income evidence: AST or, where no AST in place, a valuer's market rent assessment

### 1.4 Property
- Single residential dwellings, HMOs (max 6 beds on standard, more on Premier), multi-unit freeholds, holiday lets (Premier only)
- Excluded: ex-local-authority high rise above 5 storeys, properties with cladding requiring EWS1 of B2 grade, properties currently let to a family member

## 2. Bridging Loans

### 2.1 Eligibility
- UK residents and SPVs; foreign nationals case-by-case
- Minimum age 21; no maximum
- Exit strategies accepted: refinance to term loan (most common), sale of security, sale of other property, inheritance (with evidence)

### 2.2 Loan parameters
- Maximum LTV: 75% on residential security; 70% on commercial; 65% on land
- Maximum LTGDV on refurbishment: 70%
- Minimum loan: £100,000
- Maximum loan: £3,000,000
- Terms: 1–18 months
- Interest: retained, serviced, or rolled

### 2.3 Refurbishment criteria
- Light refurb (no planning, no structural): up to 100% of works funded
- Heavy refurb (planning required or structural): up to 100% of works in stages, subject to inspection
- All works to be carried out by approved contractor with PI cover

## 3. Commercial Mortgages

### 3.1 Asset classes accepted
- Retail (max 65% LTV), Office (max 70%), Industrial / Warehouse (max 70%), Mixed-use (max 70% — assessed on weighted basis), Care homes and student accommodation by referral only

### 3.2 Loan parameters
- Minimum loan: £150,000
- Maximum loan: £5,000,000
- Terms: 5, 10, 15 years
- Interest cover ratio: 130% minimum, 150% target

## 4. Identity, AML and Source of Funds

### 4.1 Identity verification
- Two forms of ID for each individual: one photo ID (passport or driving licence), one proof of address dated within last 3 months
- For SPVs: Companies House extract, share register, current confirmation statement, ID for all directors and 25%+ shareholders

### 4.2 AML / sanctions / PEP
- ComplyAdvantage screening mandatory before progression past FMA stage
- PEPs require Enhanced Due Diligence and senior management sign-off
- Adverse media hits reviewed case-by-case; clear documentation of treatment required

### 4.3 Source of funds
- Deposit source must be evidenced — bank statements (6 months), gift letter + donor evidence, property sale completion statement, or business accounts
- Source of wealth required for loans above £1,000,000 or where pattern is otherwise unusual

## 5. Documents required (BTL SPV — typical)

- Full Mortgage Application (FMA)
- Companies House confirmation statement and articles
- ID and proof of address for each director and 25%+ shareholder
- Personal credit search consent
- 3 months personal bank statements per director (guarantor)
- SPV bank statements (3 months) if trading
- Existing portfolio schedule with rental income and mortgage details
- AST for the security property (where in place)
- Memorandum of sale or purchase contract details
- Source of deposit evidence

## 6. Exceptions and referrals

Cases falling outside policy may proceed by exception with underwriter sign-off above the relevant authority limit:

- Up to 80% LTV (on a 75% product): Senior Underwriter
- Foreign national without ILR: Head of Underwriting
- ICR below threshold but with mitigants: Senior Underwriter
- Loan above £2,000,000 single security or £5,000,000 aggregate: Credit Committee

All exceptions logged with rationale on the credit paper.
